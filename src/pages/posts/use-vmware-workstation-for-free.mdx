## Installation
> Get vmware Workstation Pro on [vmware offical website](https://www.vmware.com/products/workstation-pro/workstation-pro-evaluation.html) (Windows,Liunx)

After installing VMware Workstation Pro go to `Help>Enter a License Key`
![enter the license key](/posts/use-vmware-workstation-for-free/navhelp.png)
Copy and paste one of the license keys below
![enter the license key](/posts/use-vmware-workstation-for-free/enterlicensekey.png)
```
ZF3R0-FHED2-M80TY-8QYGC-NPKYF
YF390-0HF8P-M81RQ-2DXQE-M2UT6
ZF71R-DMX85-08DQY-8YMNC-PPHV8
```
And that's it now you can go to`File>New Virtual Machine...`to [start a new local virtual machine](/posts/vmware-add-new-virtul-machine)
